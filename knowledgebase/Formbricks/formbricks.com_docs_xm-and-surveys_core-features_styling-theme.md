# Styling Theme

Keep the survey styling consistent over all surveys with a Styling Theme. Customize the colors, fonts, and other styling options to match your brand’s aesthetic.

**Self-Hosting Requirements**: Uploading custom background images and brand logos requires file upload
storage to be configured. If you’re self-hosting Formbricks, make sure to [configure file\\
uploads](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/configuration/file-uploads) before using these features.

Keep the survey styling consistent over all surveys with a Styling Theme. Customize the colors, fonts, and other styling options to match your brand’s aesthetic.

## [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme\#configuration)  Configuration

In the left side bar, you find the `Configuration` page. On this page you find the `Look & Feel` section:![Look & Feel](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/look-and-feel.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=7efd40ff57eaa512cbfa0cb10c8dc953)/>

## [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme\#styling-options)  **Styling Options**

1. **Form Styling:** Customize the survey card using the following settings

![Form styling options UI](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/form-settings.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=5772819dc1061c9d27bbdbdaa847e344)

- **Brand Color**: Sets the primary color tone of the survey.
- **Text Color**: This is a single color scheme that will be used across to display all the text on your survey. Ensures all text is readable against the background.
- **Input Color:** Alters the border color of input fields.
- **Input Border Color**: This is the color of the border of the form input field.

2. **Card Styling:** Adjust the look of the survey card

![Card styling options UI](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/card-settings.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=4ec405e3c9c43766e91791dc3744a34b)

- **Roundness**: Adjusts the corner roundness of the survey card and its components (including input boxes, buttons).
- **Card Background Color**: Sets the card’s main background color.
- **Card Border Color**: Changes the border color of the card
- **Hide Progress Bar**: Optionally remove the progress bar to simplify the survey experience
- **Add Highlight Border**: Adds a distinct border for emphasis.

3. **Background Styling**: Customize the survey background with static colors, animations, or images (upload your own or get from Unsplash)

This is only available for Link Surveys

![Background styling options UI](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/background-settings.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=3f4f834a21b8dbee2e42a313ae8867b1)

- **Color**: Pick any color for the background
- **Animation**: Add dynamic animations to enhance user experience..
- **Upload**: Use a custom uploaded image for a personalized touch. Images must be 5 MB or less.
- Image: Choose from Unsplash’s extensive gallery. Note that these images will have a link and mention of the author & Unsplash on the bottom right to give them the credit for their awesome work!
- **Background Overlay**: Adjust the background’s opacity

## [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme\#add-brand-logo)  Add Brand Logo

Customize your survey with your brand’s logo.

Brand logos are only visible on Link Survey pages.

1. In the Look & Feel page itself in Project settings, scroll down to see the Logo Upload box.

![Choose a link survey template](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/step-four.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=09a7acfe9c055d24f4fd2785aad6f213)

2. Upload your logo. Logos must be 5 MB or less.

![Choose a link survey template](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/step-five.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=a28323caf41515921bb9160bae89be5c)

3. Add a background color: If you’ve uploaded a transparent image and want to add background to it, enable this toggle and select the color of your choice.

![Choose a link survey template](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/step-six.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=462d0b982cff68ad43743f875b2c5476)

4. Remember to save your changes!

![Choose a link survey template](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/step-seven.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=644cf4ad055329a51ee95452f1228cc2)

The logo settings apply across all Link Surveys pages.

## [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme\#overwrite-styling-theme)  Overwrite Styling Theme

You can allow to overwrite the styling theme for individual surveys to create unique styles for each survey:![Choose a link survey template](https://mintcdn.com/formbricks/yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B/images/xm-and-surveys/core-features/styling-theme/allow-overwrite.webp?fit=max&auto=format&n=yKl9MUpEW6O1uJ5B&q=85&s=af2db5dfd5bfa39a298dfcd93a0c8033)In the survey editor, a tab called `Styling` will appear. Here you can overwrite the default styling theme.
