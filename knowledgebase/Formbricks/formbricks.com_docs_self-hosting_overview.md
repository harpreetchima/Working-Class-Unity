# Self-Hosting

Learn how to self-host Formbricks.

### [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/overview\#system-requirements)  System Requirements

**Minimum Setup**: 1 vCPU, 2 GB RAM, 8 GB SSD.

**HTTPS Required**: Ensure your server runs with a valid HTTPS certificate.

#### [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/overview\#self-hosting-options)  **Self-Hosting Options**

- [**One-Click Setup**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/setup/one-click) **:** Set up Formbricks on Ubuntu in minutes with our shell script.
- [**Docker Setup**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/setup/docker) **:** Use our pre-built Docker image for an effortless start.
- [**Migration Guide**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/advanced/migration) **:** Upgrade your existing Formbricks instance smoothly.
- [**Configuration**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/configuration) **:** Customize your setup with advanced configurations.
- [**Integrations**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/configuration/integrations) **:** Connect Formbricks to third-party tools.
- [**Licensing**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/advanced/license) **:**  Learn more about our AGPL Formbricks Core and its open-source license & Unlock advanced features tailored for larger teams and enterprises with the EE License.

### [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/overview\#hosting-options)  **Hosting Options**

- **Cloud Hosting:** Hassle-free managed deployment at [app.formbricks.com](https://app.formbricks.com/).
- **Self-Hosting Free:** Perfect for personal use or small projects.
- **Self-Hosting with EE License:** Unlock advanced features for large teams or enterprises.

### [​](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/overview\#licensing-options-comparison:)  **Licensing Options Comparison**:

For Self Hosting, our core product is free however certain advanced features are a part of our Enterprise License offering. Please refer this table to understand if you want the features that are a part of it and then we’ll reach out to you!

| **Feature** | Cloud Hosting | Self-Hosting Free | Self-Hosting EE License |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Setup & Maintenance | Managed | DIY | DIY |
| Advanced Functionality | Full (Optional Paid plans) | Most of them | All Advanced Features |
| Support & Updates | Included | Community | Prioritized |
| Price | Subscription | Free | Fixed Per month |
| Customization & Control | Limited | Moderate | Full |

For licensing queries, contact us at [**hola@formbricks.com**](https://formbricks.com/docs/self-hosting/overview).Formbricks is designed to be intuitive and accessible, ensuring an easy setup no matter which option you choose.
