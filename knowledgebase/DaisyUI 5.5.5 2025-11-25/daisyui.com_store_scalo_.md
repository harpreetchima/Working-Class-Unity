Scalo is the ultimate startup front-end template

Made with Tailwind CSS 4 and daisyUI 5

Including:

- Components and blocks
- 36 Pages
- 4 Landing pages (Automation, Developer, Sales, Storage)
- 7 Pricing sections (Lifetime, Subscription, User-based & comparison-based)
- 6 Auth pages (Login, Register, 2FA, Forgot password, Reset password, Verify)
- Settings (Profile, Account, Notification & Manage plans)
- Company pages (Team, Careers, Blogs, Timeline, Privacy & more)
- Extra pages (Coming soon page, Maintenance page, Notify & Error pages)
- Testimonial Sections (Simple + Swiper)
- FAQs + Contact Us pages
- Included with light & dark themes
- Compatible with all daisyUI themes
